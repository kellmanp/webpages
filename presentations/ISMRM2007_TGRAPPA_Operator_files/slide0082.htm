lGRAPPA-Operator approach for dynamic parallel imaging
l à Requires less adjacent time frames for obtaining reconstruction parameters
l à More robust against motion (in particular in 3D imaging) at high accelerations
l à Suited for minimizing transient response to changes when using acceleration factors of R>2
l
lWorks with conventional acquisition schemes (e.g. PE offsets 1,2,3,4,1,2,3,4,…) as well as with modified acquisition schemes (e.g. PE offsets 1,2,1,2,1,2,1,2,...)
