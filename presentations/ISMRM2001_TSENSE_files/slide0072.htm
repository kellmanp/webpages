What is TSENSE?
…. a method for accelerated MR imaging
Based on combining:
•spatial array combining (SENSE)
•temporal filtering (e.g., UNFOLD)
Applicable to:
•real time or multi-pass imaging
•retrospective cine
Benefits of approach
•adaptive
•robust design
•This talk is about a method of accelerated imaging which I refer to as TSENSE based on combining SENSE with temporal filtering, such as UNFOLD.
•There are a number of possible ways to combine these methods.
•For instance, they can be combined to achieve either:
•higher acceleration, or
•they can be combined to achieve additional ghost artifact suppression.
•I will focus on how combined spatio-temporal filtering can be used to achieve increased artifact suppression, which makes the implementation both:
•Robust, and
•Adaptive
•I will begin by discussing rate 2 acceleration, and then conclude by briefly describing the extension to higher acceleration.
•This method is applicable to:
- real-time imaging
    - such as realtime free breathing exercise stress function to be described, or
- Retrospective cine
