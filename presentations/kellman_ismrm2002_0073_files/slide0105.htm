Simplified Block Diagram
•A block diagram of the phase array ghost elimination method illustrates the processing steps.
•A sequence of complex multi-coil images are reconstructed including the images with ghosts during the initial cardiac phases.
•Ghost free images after steady state is reached are averaged and used for the estimating the B1-maps which are used in calculating the phased array combiner coefficients.
•Phased array combining is applied to the full-FOV images at each shift to separate the superimposed (ghost) images.
•The individual images are each weighted by their (space variant) point spread function value, h0(x,y), h1, h2, & h3, respectively.
•These separated images may be combined to produce a final ghost artifact cancelled image.
