Phase Sensitive Inversion Recovery for Detecting Myocardial Infarction using Gd-DTPA
Delayed Hyper-Enhancement
Peter Kellman, Andrew E. Arai, Elliot R. McVeigh, Anthony H. Aletras
Laboratory for Cardiac Energetics
National Heart, Lung, and Blood Institute
National Institutes for Health
Presented at ISMRM 2001 Glasgow, Scotland
•My talk will describe the benefits of phase sensitive reconstruction for detecting delayed hyper-enhancement of myocardial infarctions using inversion recovery with contrast agent
•Despite the fact that, there are well known benefits of phase sensitive recontruction for inversion recovery imaging, it is not used commonly in practice
•I will describe the methods we used to implement phase sensitive reconstruction and deal with the unique challenges of cardiac imaging
