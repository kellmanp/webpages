•Cardiac Imaging Application
•Comparison of Surface Coil Arrays
-  4 & 8-element arrays
-  1-d linear & 2-d area arrays
•Evaluation of image quality
-  full k-space
-  accelerated SENSE imaging (R=2,3,4)
Purpose
•The performance of parallel imaging using SENSE is highly dependent on the size, position, and number of surface coils, as well as the imaging geometry.
•We evaluated performance of surface coils arrays with several geometries for performing the SENSE method specifically for cardiac imaging.
