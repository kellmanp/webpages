Cine Imaging Parameters*
•Cardiac cine imaging was performed using an ECG gated, segmented FISP sequence.
•With the spatial resolution and other parameters used, the breath-hold duration was 24 heart beats without acceleration, and was reduced by factors of 2, 3, & 4 using SENSE.
•SENSE coefficients were estimated adaptively by the TSENSE method wherein the phase encode lines are shifted 1 line each cardiac phase to produce a ghost free reference with lower temporal resolution.
•Imaging was performed on a GE LX scanner which was custom modified for 8 channel operation using a multi-channel digital receiver.
