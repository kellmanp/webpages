Conclusions
Cardiac 3-d imaging:
• g-factor improved for 2-d vs. 1-d SENSE
• g-factor penalty greater due to acceleration along z
     -  sensitivity profiles less distinct in z-direction
     -  g-factor degrades for smaller slabs
Coil geometry:
• 2x2 arrays poor for 1-d SENSE
• linear and 2x2 arrays arrays comparable for oblique slices
• linear array more versatile (for both single or multi-slice cardiac)
