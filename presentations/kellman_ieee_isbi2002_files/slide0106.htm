Evaluation of Parallel MR (SENSE) performance
- application:…………………….cardiac volume imaging
- rate=4………………………………..1-d vs. 2-d SENSE
-  performance criteria:……….…...……..SENSE g-factor
- surface coil geometries:…………….1x4 vs. 2x2 arrays
-
Purpose of present study
•The performance of parallel MR imaging using SENSE is highly dependent on the size, position, and number of surface coils, as well as the imaging geometry.
•We evaluated performance of surface coils arrays with several geometries for performing the SENSE method for volume MR imaging with acceleration with Rate R=4.
•We considered both 1-d and 2-d SENSE, and studied the effect of oblique imaging angle on the SENSE g-factor.
