nThis study has characterized the relative contribution of systematic and random noise to the displacement, E1, and E2 calculations in cine DENSE MRI
nPreviously described pulse sequence can achieve myocardial SNR ranging from 30-10 at 3T1
nAssuming control end-systolic displacement, E1, and E2 values of 5 mm, 0.4, and -0.2, respectively, the acceptable limits of error are 0.25 mm, 0.02, and -0.01, respectively (95% CI)
nPhantom data analysis show that errors at SNR = 10 (worst case in vivo) are within the acceptable limits
nApproaches to reduce the systematic error include effective shimming and higher receiver bandwidth
