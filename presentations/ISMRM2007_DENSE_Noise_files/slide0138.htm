Introduction
nCine DENSE MRI1 is a high-resolution modality that can be used to quantify regional myocardium function
nThe noise in phase-contrast MRI is inversely proportional to the magnitude of magnetization2
nIn DENSE MRI, the noise in displacement is inversely proportional to the magnitude of magnetization, but the noise in strain is less straight forward because strain involves more complex processes (e.g., finite element model, diagonalization of strain components)
nAdditionally, in echo-combined DENSE MRI3,4, the intrinsic phase correction does not eliminate the systematic phase error due to B0 inhomogeneity
