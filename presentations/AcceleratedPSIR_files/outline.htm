Discussion
  • SENSE Method
    • g-factor related SNR loss
    •         approx. 30% for 4 coils (< 5% for 8 coils)
    • more sensitive to localizing for best in-plane rotation and to avoid wrap (canceling wrap increases g-factor loss)
  • Partial k-space Method
    • point spread function (PSF) for combined partial k-space & inversion recovery may, in theory, cause edge artifacts
    • artifacts minimized using smaller acquisition window
    • partial echo readout method has preferred PSF

Conclusion
  • R=2 accelerated phase sensitive IR is practical
  • reduced breath-hold duration or improved spatial resolution possible
  • may be combined with multi-slice phase sensitive IR for single breath-hold 8 slice SAX stack
