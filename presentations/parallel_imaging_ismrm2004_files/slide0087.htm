Example short axis cardiac images acquired at acceleration rates 2, 3, and 4 are shown before and after SENSE reconstruction. These are real-time free breathing true-FISP without ECG triggering acquired at 15, 22.5, and 30 fps for rates 2, 3, and 4 respectively, using a 128x60 acquisition matrix

The imaging parameters are as follows:
Sequence:  real-time true-FISP (no ECG trigger/gate)
Acceleration: auto-calibrating TSENSE with adaptive conditioning (without any post temporal image filtering)
R=2  15 fps
R=3  22.5 fps
R=4  30 fps
Acquisition matrix: 128x60  (81.25% phase FOV giving final 128x104 matrix of interpolated square pixels).
Readout flip angle: 50 deg flip
Number Coils: 8 (Nova Medical Inc)
TR=2.2 ms (15 phases acquired x 2.2 ms= 33ms = 1/30 fps)
Slice thickness: 8 mm
