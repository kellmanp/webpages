Griswold MA, et al, MRM. 2002 Jun;47(6):1202-10.
A further generalization of the SMASH method is to use blocks of k-space lines for calculating the missing phase encodes. This improves the ability to model coil sensitivity profiles which have sharper edges, and correspondingly greater spatial bandwidth. For example, this approach is used in the GRAPPA method [23].
