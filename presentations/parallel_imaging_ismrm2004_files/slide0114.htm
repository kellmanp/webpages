2-d SENSE Example
Acceleration Rate 8 using 32 channels*
   These images are an example of 2-d SENSE applied to cardiac cine 3d imaging. In this application, a trueFISP sequence with gated segmented acquisition is used to acquire the full 4d dataset in single 20 heartbeat breath-hold using 2-d SENSE with rate 4x2=8. Parameters for this acquisition were: TE/TR= 1.4/2.8 ms, 50 deg readout flip angle, acquisition matrix: 128 x104 x20 with 25% slice over-sampling resulting in 16 slices (4 discarded), resolution= 2.5x2.6x5 mm3, 13 views per segment producing approximately 40 ms temporal resolution.
