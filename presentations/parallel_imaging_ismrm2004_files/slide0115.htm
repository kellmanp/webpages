The calculation of EHE may be performed efficiently using FFTs [Wajer and Pruessmann]* with appropriate k-space domain weighting calculated from the sampling trajectory. Each iteration there are 2 FFTs, each oversampled corresponding to a 2x FOV.

*Wajer FTAW, Pruessmann KP, “Major speedup of reconstruction for sensitivity encoding with arbitrary trajectories,” Proc.Intl.Soc.Mag.Reson.Med, vol. 9, p. 767, 2001.
