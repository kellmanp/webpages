Iterative Solution
conjugate gradient method
Iterative least square solution [13] based on the conjugate gradient method may be used to compute solutions converging within reasonable reconstruction times. The conjugate gradient method may be applied to the so called normal equations (EHE f=EH G) after pre-whitening to de-correlate the noise. The k-space data must be re-gridded at the first iteration.
