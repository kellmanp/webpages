Methods
§A slice of the heart in the region of a wall motion abnormality was repeatedly imaged
§The inversion time was systematically changed in 25 ms increments.
§Range 75 ms to ~350 ms typically
§At least 4 acquisitions had to be less than the optimal myocardial null to be included for analysis
§Both magnitude and phase sensitive image reconstruction were performed on the same acquired data to eliminate registration errors.
