Novel approach for dynamic parallel MRI
  • Assumption: dynamic changes occur in localized regions
  • Raw-data subtraction prior to parallel MRI reconstruction
  • à Obtain dynamic information by subtracting static part (i.e. baseline) from individual time-frames
  • à Fewer pixel fold on top of each other
  • à Improved parallel MRI reconstruction (i.e. reduced geometry factor)


  • Applications:
    • Dynamic Cardiac Imaging
    • Contrast-enhanced angiography
    • fMRI
