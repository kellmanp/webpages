Typical Protocol
Gadolinium dose: 0.1-0.2 mmol/kg
time from dose: 10-20 minutes
pulse sequence: ECG gated (diastolic), segmented, turbo-FLASH
k-space acquisition: linear interleaved order
spatial resolution (typical): 1.4 x 2 x 6 mm3 (256 x 135 matrix)
TE/TR/TI: 3.9/8.5/250-300 ms
bandwidth: 140 Hz/pixel
imaging window: 145-200 ms (17-23 views per segment)
IR preparation (non-selective): 2 R-R
breath-hold duration: 14-18 heart beats/slice (including 2 HB discarded acq)
RF flip angle: 25° IR image (5° reference)
Kim RJ, et al., JCMR. 2003;5(3):505-514.
