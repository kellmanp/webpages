Artifact Cancellation using SENSE Phased Array Processing
Peter Kellman and Elliot R. McVeigh
Laboratory for Cardiac Energetics
National Heart, Lung, and Blood Institute
National Institutes for Health
Presented at ISMRM 2001 Glasgow, Scotland
•I will present a method for ghost artifact cancellation based on phased array combining, which is similar to the method known as SENSE used for accelerated imaging.
•I will show results that demonstrate the use and benefits of this method in a cardiac imaging application.
