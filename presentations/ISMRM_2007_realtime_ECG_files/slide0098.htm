Methods: Sequence Design and Implementation
§Complete scheme is designed and integrated on a clinical scanner (MAGNETOM Avanto, Siemens, Erlangen, Germany).
§TrueFISP based pulse sequence is modified to synchronously retrieve ECG and respiratory signal values from physiological monitoring unit (PMU) before every readout event.
§Physiological signal information is then sent to the image calculation environment (ICE, Siemens, Erlangen, Germany) along with each line of k-space data.
