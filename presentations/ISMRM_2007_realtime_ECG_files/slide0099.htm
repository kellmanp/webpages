Methods: Image Reconstruction
§During image reconstruction ECG/Respiratory data is embedded into Cine MR images in following three steps:
1)PMUBufferFunctor: buffers the ECG and/or respiratory signal values into memory.
2)ImageBufferFunctor: buffers reconstructed images of individual cardiac phases into memory.
3)PMUOverlayFunctor: retrieves buffered physiological and image data after all cardiac phases for a slice are acquired, and constructs additional image series with embedded ECG/respiratory traces as graphical overlays.
