§Applications:
§Assessment of dyskinesis through synchronized ECG display.
§Enhanced evaluation of wall motion abnormalities through true ECG indications of events within the cardiac cycle.
§Monitoring of arrhythmia and its impact on image quality.
§Monitoring heart rate changes with different respiratory states – e.g. autonomic reflex assessment in the Valsalva maneuver. §Retrospective k-space editing based on irregular physiological trace patterns such as arrhythmia (already in use with CT to improve image quality).

§Assumption: PMU provides reasonably accurate signal information without significant drifts or delays.
§Magneto-hydrodynamic effect distorts the ECG-signal.
