These are the typical parameters we use for real time imaging. 3 different matrix sizes are available for use, resulting in different temporal resolution and corresponding frame rates.  We typically use the 192 X80 matrix with approximately 50 ms temporal resolution or 20 frames per second. While this represents a compromise from what may be achieved with segmented cine, it has the benefit of not requiring ECG or breath-holding.
Our images were obtained on Siemens Avanto 1.5T magnet with 32 channels.
