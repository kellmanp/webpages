Another benefit of real-time imaging is the ability to acquire many slices quickly by eliminating the pauses between breath-holds. We obtained this entire stack of short axis images in a single free-breathing 20 second acquisition. In this case we chose to acquire a couple of cardiac cycles per slice. These images were obtained with a 160 X 90 matrix using the 32 channel coil.
