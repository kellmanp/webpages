Why real time?  With the varying techniques currently available for cardiac imaging , what is our motivation for real time imaging?
We want  a non ECG triggered, free breathing method of imaging, that will avoid triggering problems in pts with arrhythmia.  We also want  a rapid acquisition to avoid  breathing artifacts in heart failure and  pediatric patients who can’t hold their breath.
