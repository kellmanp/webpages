The performance of the SENSE method of parallel imaging relies on the number and geometry of the coils. We used custom surface coil arrays to improve performance in order to work at 4-to-1 accelerations. We used both 8 and 32 channel custom arrays. The 32 element coil, with 4 X4 anterior and posterior arrays, from Invivo Corp is shown here.  We also use an 8 element cardiac array from Nova Medical Systems, which could be used on either the Sonata or Avanto.
