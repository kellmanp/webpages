The Volterra model is a functional expansion of the output in terms of multi-dimensional convolutions of varying orders.
It is customary to truncate the model to 2nd order due to practical measurement limitations.
The variables k0, k1, k2,  in the functional expansion are referred to as the Volterra kernels. Example 1st and 2nd order kernels are shown for illustration. Note that the 2nd order kernel weights lag products of the form x(t-n1)x(t-n2).
