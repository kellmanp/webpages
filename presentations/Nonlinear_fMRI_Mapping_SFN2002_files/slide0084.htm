•previously used for measuring of electrical response to visual stimuli*
* Sutter (1987), Benardete & Victor (1994)
•M-sequence probing has been used for systems identification in many applications and, in particular, has been used for electrical measurement of the non-linear response to visual activation.
•M-sequence probes have a reduced statistical fluctuation as compared with white Gaussian noise, and thereby an improved estimation efficiency.
