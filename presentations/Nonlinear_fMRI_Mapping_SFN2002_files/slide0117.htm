•The inverse repeat method measures the response to both the probe sequence and its inverse where a 1 becomes a 0 and 0 becomes a 1.
•The 1st order response changes sign proportional to the sign of the input while the 2nd order response does not change sign.
•Therefore, the odd and even order responses are separated by a simple difference and sum.
