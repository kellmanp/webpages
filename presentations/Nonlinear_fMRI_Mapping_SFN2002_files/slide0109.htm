Wiener model & kernel estimates
 using cross-correlation method
orthogonalization of Volterra functional series with white Gaussian noise stimulus:
•The Wiener model is based on the Volterra functional expansion. Wiener kernels of varying orders are orthogonalized for a white Gaussian noise probe.
•The Wiener kernels may be estimated directly using the correlation method by Lee and Schetzen, which cross-correlates the measured response with the white Gaussian probe stimulus. The 2nd order kernel is estimated with a higher order correlation.
