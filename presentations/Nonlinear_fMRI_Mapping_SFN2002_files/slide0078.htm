Can BOLD* fMRI be used to measure non-linear neuronal responses?  YES
•In conclusion, we have demonstrated that BOLD fMRI may indeed be used to probe neuronal non-linearities despite the seconds long hemodynamic response.
•This new method uses a m-sequence probe with and without a small decorrelating delay to discriminate the effects of fast and slow responses.
•The preliminary experiments with visual activation are consistent with electrical activity measured using a variety of methods.
•The increased temporal contrast sensitivity in the periphery has also been reported.
