                     Orientation:                         LAX

                     Sequence:                           real-time True-FISP

                                                               non-ECG triggered

                                                               normal free breathing

                     Matrix:                               128x88

                     FOV:                                 300 x 282 mm2

                     Resolution:                          2.3 x 3.2 mm2

                     Slice thickness:                   8 mm

                     Readout flip angle:              55 deg

                     TR:                                     2.3 ms

                     Bandwidth:                         1395 Hz/pixel

                     Acquisition duration:           approx. 10 sec each

                     Acceleration:                      TSENSE Parallel Imaging method (rates 1 thru 4)

                                                               auto-calibrating TSENSE

                                                               without UNFOLD temporal filtering

                     Scanner:                             1.5T Siemens Sonata

                     Coils:                                  8 element surface coil array (Nova Medical, Inc.)
