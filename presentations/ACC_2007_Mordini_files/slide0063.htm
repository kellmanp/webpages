Methods
•67 patients with known or suspected coronary artery disease with a clinical indication for a stress test.
•Dipyridamole stress perfusion (0.56 mg/kg IV over 4 minutes)
•Dual bolus protocol (0.005 mmol/kg / 0.1 mmol/kg Gd-DTPA)
•Saturation recovery, echoplanar perfusion sequence
•Subendocardial stress perfusion was quantified (ml/min/g) with Fermi function deconvolution.
•MRI scans were also interpreted qualitatively clinically and using the Duke Algorithm (both blind to patient history and QCA).
